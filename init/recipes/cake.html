Ice Cream Cake

Description

Learn how to make a delicious ice cream cake with a mix of chocolate, vanilla, cookie crumble, topped with frosting and oreos.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Ice Cream
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Oreos
  • Frosting

Steps

  1. Crush up half your cookies
  2. Freeze your ice cream
  3. Take a circular shaped, pan and fill it halfway with vanilla ice cream
  4. Add a layer of the cookie crumble
  5. Fill the rest with chocolate ice cream
  6. Add the frosting and Oreos on top
